About

Hi, I am Pareekshith. 
I would describe myself as highly curious and focused on learning in all parts of life, personal and professional. I look for the hardest problems to solve and where I can learn and develop the most. 
I’ve taken on many different types of projects, including Android, Web, Commerce and Cloud Computing. No matter what I’m working on, I’m very invested. I identify anyone I can learn from, as well as problems that I care about and try to optimize every step of the process. 

In my personal life, I spend a lot of time on photography, i love to capture nature and wildlife and have a focused area of interest for a longer period of time. 
Outside of reading and photography, I also love to travel, cook with friends, and fitness enthusiast and being physically active outdoors.
